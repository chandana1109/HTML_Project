About Japan

Japan is a major contributor to its economy through tourism, attracting 31.88 million international tourists in 2019. In 2021, Japan's tourism sector generated $5.23 billion, which is 0.12% of its GDP.
Japan is known for its Buddhist temples, Shinto shrines, and unique culture, including traditional arts, fashion, cuisine, and historical sites. The country also has diverse landscapes, including the snowy peaks of Mount Fuji and the beaches of Okinawa.
Here are some other reasons to visit Japan:
